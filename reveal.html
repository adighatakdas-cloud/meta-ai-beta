🎣 What Happened?

🍪 Cookie Theft

We didn't just get the password. We captured session cookies — the "golden tickets" that keep users logged in. With these, an attacker can impersonate them indefinitely.

🛡️ How to Protect

Always check the URL before entering credentials. Use password managers. Enable hardware security keys for 2FA — they can't be phished like SMS codes.
